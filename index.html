Our story

Born in Tel Aviv,
built for Tel Aviv.

We spent months navigating the Tel Aviv rental market ourselves — calling agents, losing apartments to faster movers, paying fees for nothing. It was exhausting.

So we built the tool we wished existed. Baita is a direct marketplace where landlords post their apartments and seekers find their home — with messaging built in, verified profiles, and a beautiful experience on both sides.
